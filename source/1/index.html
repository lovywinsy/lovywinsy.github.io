Engineer not Developer¶

I have attended a one-week training about TDD, one of the most impressed thing is everytime when we said developer the trainer would correct it with engineer.

Obviously he thought developer directly determine the work of others, it’s not polite at all.

Compare to developer, engineer is more accurate to describe what we are. And I believe that he must know another title in China, Code Farmer. I have to say it’s really a bad title, it’s not polite and it will make everyone’s living environment worse slowly and slowly, but actually lots of people don’t realize it now.
